The Story

A Passion for Visual Storytelling

Hi, I'm Kofi Mensah — a Ghanaian photographer based in Accra with over 8 years of experience turning fleeting moments into enduring art. My journey began with a borrowed camera and a curiosity about light, shadow, and the human face.

Today, Babphotography serves clients across Ghana and West Africa — from intimate portrait sessions to large-scale editorial campaigns and destination weddings. Every shoot is crafted with intention, care, and a deep respect for the story we are telling together.

When I'm not behind the lens, you'll find me exploring Accra's streets, scouting golden-hour locations, or mentoring young photographers who share the same obsession with beautiful light.
